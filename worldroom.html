Can AI help me to understand “the outside” from “the inside”? In this project I built a database with photos of my room, trained a Machine Learning model, and finally went outside after more than 30 days of quarantine, to write down what it feels to be free again. In the end, I set a video installation in Unity that tries to reflect how I experienced the “new normal” after all that insecurity, lost, loneliness and self-exploration.
