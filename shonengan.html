The Weekly Shonen Jump is the magazine that has been publishing all the famous mangas like Dragon Ball, One Piece or Naruto. I used the 52 anniversary of the magazine as an excuse and I trained a GAN model with every Shonen Jump magazine cover since 1968 (around 2600 issues), and I created an infinite generator of covers that replicates the general aesthetics of the magazine.
